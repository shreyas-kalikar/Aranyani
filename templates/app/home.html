The relentless rise of carbon dioxide

Ancient air bubbles trapped in ice enable us to step back in time and see what Earth's atmosphere, and climate, were like in the distant past. We know, for a fact that CO2 levels in the atmosphere are at an all-time high in 400000 years. Beginning from 200ppm in the Ice Age, the CO2 levels have reached a recorded high of 400ppm in 2013. This recent relentless rise in CO2 shows a remarkably constant relationship with fossil-fuel burning and increasing urbanisation. If this is not stopped now, it may well be too late and CO2 levels might reach as high as 1500ppm in the coming centuries.

Carbon dioxide hits new high

Think of a future where climate change has taken an irreversible toll on us. The Earth is a hotter and wetter place. The sea level is rising at a constant rate, with upto 40 meters of increase in water levels, and there is a 3 to 4 degree Celsius increase in temperature globally, except with a drastic increase of 10 degree Celsius on the north and south pole.

How it works

FUNDING SYSTEM

After the donation made by you is processed, you will recieve certification stating your contribution, making the process fully transparent and up front. The money donated will be delegated to multiple verified NGOs (or) the local garden municipality, depending on the region. If the plant report is not submitted to you withing 3 months, you will be refunded the money, where you can choose to possibly replant in another region.

FOR PLANTER

Can't donate? Not a problem! You can still help the environment by contributing to a tree entity being funded by others like you! You can donate a small denomination to even partially cover the charge by filling an application that vouches for your donation, and the other terms stand the same!

FOR DONATOR

For donations, we have quick, accessible, and secure portals where you can donate through online payment methods such as credit, debit, or UPI! This is the money that will be sent to NGOs that will take the process of plantation forward in the region of your choice.

Wood Store Locator

Our site would also connect one to the nearest wood storage facility in case of need of wood. This feature would inform anyone in need of wood with the nearest wood storage facility, it's availability, and the contact details for the same. This would be a huge step forward and would avoid a lot of unnecessary cutting of trees.
